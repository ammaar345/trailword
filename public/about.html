About TrailWord

A small, free word puzzle made for a daily five-minute break.

What TrailWord is

TrailWord is a free daily word puzzle you play in your browser. Each day brings one new five-letter word to uncover, framed by a category and a clue so the puzzle feels like following a trail rather than guessing in the dark. It takes a couple of minutes, works on phones and desktops, and asks for nothing — no sign-up, no download, no personal details.

Why we built it

Daily word games are a lovely ritual, but many of them drop you in front of a blank grid with zero context. TrailWord adds a category and a written clue to every puzzle so newcomers have a foothold and veterans can chase a faster solve. The word list spans more than a thousand answers across themes like Nature, Food, Science, People, Places, and Ideas, which keeps the daily puzzle feeling varied over the long run.

What is inside

  • A Daily puzzle that is identical for every player and resets at midnight UTC.
  • An unlimited Practice mode for warming up with no guess cap.
  • Custom trails so you can turn any five-letter word into a puzzle to share.
  • Gentle, limited hints that guide without giving the answer away.
  • Streaks, win rates, and a guess-distribution graph, stored only on your device.
  • Five contrast themes, adjustable text size, optional sound, and a colorblind-friendly mode.

Privacy and how it stays free

TrailWord has no accounts and stores your progress only in your browser. The site is kept free through unobtrusive advertising and optional hint packs; nothing about your gameplay is sold. You can read the full privacy policy for the details on storage, analytics, and advertising partners.
