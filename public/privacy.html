Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026

What TrailWord stores

TrailWord has no accounts and no sign-up. Your game data — statistics, streaks, current board, and settings — is stored only in your browser's localStorage on your device. It is never sent to our servers. Clearing your browser data deletes it.

Third-party services

  • Cloudflare Pages hosts this site and may log standard technical request data (IP address, user agent) for security and delivery. See the Cloudflare privacy policy.
  • Free Dictionary API (dictionaryapi.dev) is queried for word definitions after a game ends. Only the finished puzzle word is sent — nothing about you.
  • Google Fonts serves the site's fonts; your browser requests font files from Google's servers.

Advertising

TrailWord may display ads served by Google AdSense. Google and its partners may use cookies or device identifiers to show ads based on your visits to this and other sites. You can opt out of personalised advertising at Google Ads Settings, and learn more at Google's advertising policies.

Purchases

Optional hint packs are sold through Gumroad. Payment is handled entirely by Gumroad — TrailWord never sees your payment details. See the Gumroad privacy policy.
